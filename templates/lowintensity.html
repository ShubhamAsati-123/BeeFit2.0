Low-intensity steady-state (LISS) training

Some tips for Liss training are starting with a short workout and gradually increase the duration as you get fitter and stop the exercise if you feel any pain. Some of the benefits of LISS training are improved cardiovascular health,blood sugar control and mental health.It helps burn calories and reduces weight.It reduces the risk of chronic diseases and stress levels.
