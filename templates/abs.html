ABS EXERCISE

Abdominal muscles are responsible for many important functions like keeping the spine in alignment and preventing back pain, helps to expand and contract the lungs during breathing and helps to stabilize the core which is important for activities like lifting weights and playing sports. Doing abs exercise strengthens the abdominal muscle, reduces risk of injury, improves mood and athletic performance, increases metabolism and reduces the back pain. As always warm up before you start exercising and cool down after you finish your exercise. Hydrate properly and take a healthy diet. Vary your routine and get enough rest.
