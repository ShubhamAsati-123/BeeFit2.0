ARM EXERCISE

Arm exercises increases muscle mass which can improve your appearance and give you stronger arms, it improves strength which can be helpful for everyday activities. It also reduces the risk of injury as strong muscles are less likely to be injured than weaker muscles. One should warm up before starting the workout, use a weight that is challenging but not too heavy and keep a proper form to reduce the risk of injuries. Cool down after your workout.
