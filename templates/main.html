Exercises

Types of Exercises

Workout, or exercise is a term often encountered by individuals of all ages. It refers to a wide spectrum of physical activities which give your body a boost of oxygen, blood flow, dopamine and serotonin, making it a great method of maintaining both physical and mental health.

Some of the most popularly practiced forms of exercise are aerobics, cardio workout, flexibility exercises, pilates, etc. Here at BEEFIT, you have the opportunity to explore the various forms of exercise, in a matter of few clicks!

Diet

Different Types of Deit Plan

The key to a healthy body is eating 'right'. This implies following a diet plan constituting of nutritious edibles and a balanced diet, formulated for your fitness journey!

Diets definitely don't follow the one-size-fits-all property, and depend on various contributing factors including but not limited to metabolism rates, age, levels of physical activity, etc. Keep reading to find multiple diet plans that you can choose based on your choice of food!

Tracking

Tracking Your Calorie Intake and Burnt

Did you know? The amount of calories you are advised to intake corresponds to the amount of physical activity you perform on a daily basis. Based on your fitness goal, i.e. weight loss/weight gain/general fitness, the amount of calories to be consumed differs.

Our calorie tracker you can measure the amount of calories you are consuming, and the amount of calories that you burn, hence giving you the net calorie consumption.
