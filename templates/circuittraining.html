CIRCUIT TRAINING

To start the circuit, begin with the first exercise and perform it for the prescribed number of repetitions or time. Then, immediately move on to the next exercise and repeat. Continue until you have completed all of the exercises in the circuit.Once you have completed the circuit, take a short rest (30-60 seconds) and then repeat the circuit for the desired number of sets.Some tips for circuit training are as followed: Warming up before you start your workout,using a weight that is challenging but not too heavy,performing each exercise with good form,taking short rests between exercises and drinking plenty of water before, during, and after your workout. Some of the benifits of circuit training are improving cardiovascular fitness,increasing strength and endurance,improving flexibility and mood and reducing stress.
