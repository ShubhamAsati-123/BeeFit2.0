This category involves some specific body parts which are highly targeted during core exercise.The category focuses on chest, back, shoulders, arms, legs and back exercises. The further sub category describes more about their benefits, importance and also consist of video resources which provide the best exercises related to a specific body part.

Choose the body part upon which you want to workout

Chest Exercise

Chest exercises are a great way to build muscle and strength in the chest area. There are many different chest exercises that can be done, both with and without weights.Some of the most popular chest exercises are different types of dumbbell press and pushups.

Back Exercise

Back exercises are a great way to improve your posture, prevent back pain, and build muscle mass. Some of the most common back exercise are pull-ups, deadlifts and bent over rows.

Shoulder Exercise

Shoulder exercises are a great way to improve your posture, prevent shoulder pain, and build muscle mass. There are many different types of shoulder exercises like lateral raises, front raises,bent over rows and many more, so you can find ones that are appropriate for your fitness level and goals.

Arms Exercise

Arms exercises are a great way to tone and strengthen your biceps, triceps, and forearms. There are many different types of arms exercises like bicep curls, triceps extensions, forearm curls and many more, so you can find ones that are appropriate for your fitness level and goals.

Leg Exercise

Leg exercises are a great way to improve your overall fitness and health.Leg exercises can be done without no equipments or one can also join gym.Different types of leg exercises are squats, lunges, deadlifts, leg presses, leg extensions and many more.

Abs Exercise

Abs exercises are a type of physical activity that targets the abdominal muscles.Some of the common abs exercises are crunches, leg raises, planks, hanging leg lifts and many more.
