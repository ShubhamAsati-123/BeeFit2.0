SHOULDER EXERCISE

Shoulder exercise improves our body posture, increases the muscle mass and effectively reduces the shoulder pain, thereby increasing the strength of the shoulder. Before starting any of the shoulder exercise one should warm up which helps the body to prevent injuries, use a weight that is challenging but not too heavy and focus on the proper form which will help produce results in more faster way and will prevent the risk of injury also. After workout one should cool down and take rest so as to bring the body back to normal state.
